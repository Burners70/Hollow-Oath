✦ lineage

A love letter to the 16-bit lander classics

If you grew up steering a lander through cramped 16-bit caves, fighting gravity one careful thrust at a time. Trying not to crash. Trying not to lose the cargo you came for. This is built in that lineage. Hollow Oath is a spiritual successor to games like Oids (Atari ST, 1987), Thrust, and Gravitar. Original code, art, story, and names throughout, unaffiliated with any of them, but built by someone who loved them.

Privacy

Hollow Oath collects no personal data. There is no account, no analytics SDK, no ad network, and no in-app purchase system.

  • Your run progress, high score, and codex entries are saved locally on your device.
  • If you're signed into iCloud, that save data syncs privately through Apple's iCloud key-value storage to your other devices — Apple's iCloud privacy terms apply, and we never see this data.
  • If you connect Game Center, leaderboard and achievement data is handled entirely by Apple under Game Center's own privacy terms.

No data is sent to us, sold, or shared with third parties, because none is collected in the first place.
